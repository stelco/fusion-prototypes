From mazy, exotic markets and trackless golden deserts to luxury spas, the Middle East’s charms know few boundaries. Dubai and its iconic skyline is a far cry from the region’s more traditional heartlands, but anyone who has graced the City of Gold will tell you it appeals in equal measures. That’s the beauty of the Middle East – the variety. ...

Zighy Bay encompasses everything you’d want from a beach retreat, laced with a fine dosing of opulence for good measure. Once you’re there, gaze out onto the Gulf of Oman as you unwind with palm trees and white sands, relishing a more soothing side to Middle East holidays.

Abu Dhabi, the capital of the UAE, aligns cultural and historic artefacts as one. Highlights here include the pearlescent Sheikh Zayed Grand Mosque, made up of lavish turrets and floral carpets. Spiritual sites like this rub shoulders with plate glass towers, overlooking swanky yachts which breeze effortlessly in from neighbouring countries. Fellow emirates like Ajman house everything from 18th century fortresses to secluded beaches, with the occasional camel ride. To this day, it remains one of the world’s most fascinating and complex destinations – and you’ll love every second of uncovering it.
